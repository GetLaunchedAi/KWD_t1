Custom Home Additions in Michigan by Downs Construction

Expand Your Space with Confidence and Quality

At Downs Construction, we specialize in designing and building custom home additions that blend seamlessly with your existing home while maximizing space, functionality, and value. Whether you're adding a new bedroom, family room, or a complete second story, our team works closely with you from concept to completion.

Why Add a Home Addition?

  1. Create more living space without moving
  2. Boost property value with strategic upgrades
  3. Accommodate growing families or new lifestyle needs
  4. Enhance home comfort, efficiency, and flow

Types of Additions We Build

Every addition is tailored to fit your home’s style and your family’s needs. Our capabilities include:

  • Room additions (bedrooms, living rooms, home offices)
  • Second-story additions
  • Garage conversions and expansions
  • Sunrooms and enclosed porches
  • In-law suites and multi-generational spaces
